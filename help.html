Welcome to Alien Annihilation! The basic gist is to kill aliens and move up levels. Players will have to kill at least 70% of aliens on a single level in order for a level to be considered cleared. Each level will contain a door that will lead you to the next level. Using the doors will move you up levels, regardless of how many enemies you've killed, but there will be consequeneces if a level hasn't been cleared. You cannot move down levels. On the third level, there will be a boss that needs to be defeated. Rooms contain loot that will aid you on your journey. Note that you will need to aim manually with your mouse.
